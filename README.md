# EcoPulse AI 🌱

## Carbon Footprint Awareness Platform

EcoPulse AI is a sustainability platform developed for Google PromptWars 2026 Challenge 3.

The platform helps users understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through personalized insights and sustainability analytics.

## Features

* Carbon Footprint Calculator
* Eco Score System
* Sustainability Levels & Rankings
* Sustainability Goals Tracking
* Activity History Management
* AI Sustainability Assistant
* Analytics Dashboard
* Carbon Emission Trends
* Sustainability Insights
* PDF Sustainability Reports
* Achievement Badge System
* Interactive 3D Globe Visualization

## How It Works

1. Users enter transportation distance and electricity usage.
2. Carbon emissions are calculated automatically.
3. Eco Score and Sustainability Rank are generated.
4. Personalized recommendations are provided.
5. Activity history is stored locally.
6. Analytics dashboard visualizes emission trends.
7. Sustainability reports can be exported as PDF.
